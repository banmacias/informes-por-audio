# Workflow: Monday Team Meeting

## Objective
Record and document the Monday team meeting where therapists discuss all patients and next steps. This complements (not replaces) the physical post-it system.

## Context
- Every Monday, the therapy team meets to discuss all kids they help
- Currently they use physical post-its on patient records
- This app provides a digital backup and structured summary

## Steps
1. One team member opens the app and starts a new recording
2. Selects "Reunión de equipo" as session type
3. Enters title like "Reunión equipo - [date]"
4. Records the entire meeting
5. After the meeting, taps stop and uploads
6. App transcribes and generates a structured meeting summary organized by patient
7. Team reviews and edits the summary
8. Summary can be shared via email to all team members

## Report Template (Team Meeting)
The AI generates a report with these sections:
- **Pacientes Discutidos**: Each patient mentioned, with brief summary
- **Acuerdos y Decisiones**: Agreements made during the meeting
- **Seguimientos Pendientes**: Action items, who is responsible, deadlines
- **Notas Adicionales**: Other relevant points

## Notes
- This workflow handles longer recordings (could be 30-60+ minutes)
- The report is organized by patient when possible
- Team members can each receive a copy via email
- The digital record supplements but does not replace physical post-its
